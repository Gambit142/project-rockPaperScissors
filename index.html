Project Rock Paper Scissors

This game titled Rock, Paper and Scissors will be played between you the player and the computer.

Instructions
  • Input your object (rock, paper, scissors) in the box provided.
  • Please note that 'rock beats scissors', 'scissors beats paper' and 'paper beats rock'.
  • The above instruction means that if you choose rock and computer chooses scissors, you win and vice versa.
  • The first to reach five (5) wins the game.
  • To play the game again, press the RELOAD button
  • Goodluck!
